About.


Life Changing Labs ("LCL") is a startup network which currently supports 8 startups with 40+ students year round including highly dedicated engineers, designers, MBAs, JDs, and PhDs. By providing early first-hand experience at creating value from the ground up, LCL fosters the startup mentality within its talented members.

Our Summer Program lasts ten weeks, giving students first-hand knowledge into the basics of launching a new venture. We are very hands-on at this stage, making sure participants receive proper guidance and are positioned best for success.

We do not believe in the "one-size fits all" approach which is why we have value adds for founders and startups at all developmental stages!

Throughout the year, we provide access to an ever growing network which includes experienced entrepreneurs and graduates of LCL at Google, Amazon, Palantir, Microsoft, and VC-backed startups. Startups within our network not only benefit from knoweledge exchange but also legal advice, finance help, strategy consulting and more.
