Opportunities.


Do you want to start a company this summer rather than work for one?

We’re looking for talented programmers, designers, and marketing/sales gurus for the highly competitive 2014 Summer Associate Program. Selected associates get to be a part of a founding team, enjoy free lunches at restaurants in Collegetown, and work on meaningful projects while acquiring valuable skills. The startup you’re a part of will receive additional benefits equaling thousands of dollars in value.

Benefits & Perks

  • Full service program assisting with recruiting, marketing, designing, accelerator placement.
  • Be part of a founding team and enjoy free lunches with the teammates.
  • Branding expertise and guidance from top NYC firm, Red Antler.
  • Find co-founders & business partners.
  • Access to top entrepreneur mentors and advisors.
  • Cost of servers, hosting, domains, etc. all taken care of…so you can focus on the fun part – creating!
  • Connection to portfolio companies of VCs.
  • Experience startup culture and learn from experienced founders!
